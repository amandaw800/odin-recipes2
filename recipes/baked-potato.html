Baked Potato

Description

Yummy loaded baked potato. Extremely easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Bacon bits
  • Cheese
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Get your ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 360 F
  3. Wash your potato
  4. Poke holes into your potatos
  5. Bake for 1 hour
  6. Add your favorite toppings
